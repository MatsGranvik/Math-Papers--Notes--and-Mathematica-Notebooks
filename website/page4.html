$$1^{ -\frac{1}{2}+f i }+2^{ -\frac{1}{2}+f i }+3^{ -\frac{1}{2}+f i }+4^{ -\frac{1}{2}+f i }+...= \frac{1}{\sqrt{1}} \cdot 1^{ f i }+\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \cdot 2^{ f i }+\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \cdot 3^{ f i }+\frac{1}{\sqrt{4}} \cdot 4^{ f i }+...= \sum_{t=1}^\infty \frac{1}{\sqrt{t}} \cdot \cos( f \log t ) + i \cdot \sum_{t=1}^\infty \frac{1}{\sqrt{t}} \cdot \sin( f \log t)$$

$$1^{ -\frac{1}{2} + 14.134... i }+2^{ -\frac{1}{2} + 14.134... i }+3^{ -\frac{1}{2} + 14.134... i }+4^{ -\frac{1}{2} + 14.134... i }+...$$

In fact, here it is. What do you suppose this sum equals?

$$y=\sum_{t=1}^{\lfloor x \rfloor} \frac{1}{\sqrt{t}} \cos( 14.134... \cdot \log t ) \,\,\,\,\,\,\,\,\textrm{vs}\,\,\,\,\,\,\,\, y=\int_0^x \frac{1}{\sqrt{t}} \cos( 14.134... \cdot \log t ) \,dt$$
